## ProQuest Command
(psychology AND aviation) AND (at.exact("Article") AND stype.exact("Scholarly Journals") AND la.exact("ENG") AND PEER(yes))

## Search Strategy:
• Uses basic "psychology AND aviation" search terms
• Relies on database indexing to find relevant content

## Quality Filters Applied:
• **Article Type (at.exact("Article"))** - ensures only journal articles are included
• **Source Type (stype.exact("Scholarly Journals"))** - excludes popular media and trade publications
• **Peer Review (PEER(yes))** - guarantees academic rigor and quality control
• **Language (la.exact("ENG"))** - English publications only
